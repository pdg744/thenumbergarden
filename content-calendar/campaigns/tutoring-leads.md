# Campaign: Tutoring Leads

## Goal

Generate qualified tutoring and ongoing-support leads who book a free family
visit.

## Primary CTA

Book a free family visit:

`https://thenumbergarden.com/family-visit/`

## Secondary link

Tutoring landing page:

`https://thenumbergarden.com/portland-math-tutor/`

## Positioning

The Number Garden offers a warmer, deeper kind of math support: part tutoring,
part enrichment, part confidence-building, and part invitation into a better
relationship with mathematics.

## Inclusion language

- Some students come because math is their favorite subject. Some come because
  it has not felt good in a while. Both are welcome.
- For students who need more challenge, more confidence, or simply a different
  relationship with math.
- The goal is not to sort children into math people and not-math people. The
  goal is to help each child find a real way in.

## Content angles

- Confidence without condescension.
- Challenge without pressure.
- More room to think.
- A family visit as the first step, not a package menu.
- Ongoing support chosen with care after meeting the child.

## Inclusion language

Use this often:

> We welcome students who already love math, and students who do not love math yet.

Variants:

- A camp for students who already love puzzles, and students who are still
  waiting for math to feel interesting.
- You do not have to think of yourself as a math kid to belong here.
- Already love math? Come go deeper. Not sure yet? Come play.
- No speed contest, no worksheet race. Just games, puzzles, patterns, and room
  to think.

## Content angles

- What happens at camp: games, puzzles, patterns, conversation, beautiful
  problems.
- Who camp is for: math-loving students and students who need a different way
  in.
- Why camp is different: serious math through play, not school catch-up or
  worksheets.
- Parent trust: small studio, warm adult guidance, room to think.
- Practical reminder: dates, ages, times, registration.

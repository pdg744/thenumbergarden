# Campaign: Joyful Math

## Goal

Build trust, recognition, and warmth around The Number Garden's point of view:
math can be rigorous, playful, beautiful, and humane.

## Primary CTA

Soft CTA to camp during June and July:

`https://thenumbergarden.com/portland-math-camp/`

Secondary CTA to tutoring:

`https://thenumbergarden.com/portland-math-tutor/`

## Core message

Mathematics is not just answer-getting. It is noticing, arranging, testing,
wondering, explaining, and making patterns that hold together.

## Inclusion language

- Joyful math is not only for children who already feel good at math.
- Students can enter through curiosity, play, conversation, confidence, or
  challenge.
- We welcome students who already love math, and students who do not love math
  yet.

# The Number Garden Content Calendar

This folder is the editorial source of truth for organic social publishing.
Meta Business Suite is the publishing home base, but planning, approvals,
captions, CTAs, and asset references live here first.

## Weekly rhythm

- 2 posts
- 2 stories
- 2 reels

Until August camp demand is resolved, the primary conversion goal is August
math camp registration. Tutoring and family-visit leads remain the secondary
conversion goal.

## Operating model

- Planning: Markdown calendar entries define the week, format, campaign, goal,
  caption, CTA, destination URL, status, and asset references.
- Assets: source content, creative renderers, generated stills, video-intro
  exports, reel-family outputs, and print collateral stay in their natural repo
  locations.
- Publishing packets: approved items may be assembled into human-ready folders
  with final captions, platform notes, links, and chosen assets.
- Automation bridge: a future Make.com workflow can read approved packets or an
  exported approval table, fetch hosted media, and prepare or publish to
  Facebook/Instagram.
- Publishing: actual publishing stays manual until the workflow has proven
  itself. Later automation should trigger only from an explicit approved status.

## Voice principles

- Welcome students who already love math and students who do not love math yet.
- Avoid implying The Number Garden is only for already-confident or gifted math
  students.
- Pair rigor with warmth: serious mathematics without a pressure-performance
  vibe.
- Emphasize games, puzzles, patterns, conversation, confidence, and room to
  think.
- Avoid worksheet-race language, speed-as-status language, and generic tutoring
  marketplace language.
- Make the invitation concrete enough for parents to understand what to do next.

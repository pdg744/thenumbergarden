August Math Camp at The Number Garden is for students who already love math, and students who do not love math yet.

We will spend the week with games, puzzles, patterns, conversation, and beautiful problems. No worksheet race, no speed contest. Just serious, joyful math with room to think.

August 10-14 in Portland.
Ages 8-10: 9-11am
Ages 11-15: 1-3:30pm

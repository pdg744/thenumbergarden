# Reel Script: Puzzle Prompt

Working concept: a simple puzzle prompt reel that can be made from phone footage
of hands/cards/paper, or from the static cover image as a minimal animated text
reel.

## Visual plan

```text
0-2s: Show the numbers 3, 3, 8, 8 on cards or paper.
2-5s: Ask: Can you make 24?
5-7s: Add: No pressure. Just play.
7-9s: August Math Camp: games + puzzles for curious students.
```

## Overlay text

```text
Can you make 24
using 3, 3, 8, 8?

No pressure.
Just play.
```

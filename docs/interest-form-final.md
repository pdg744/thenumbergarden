# Google Form Copy: Interest Form

This is the recommended version-one public form for The Number Garden.

It is designed to be:

- low friction
- family-centered
- warm but clear
- short enough to complete quickly

## Form title

`The Number Garden: Interest Form`

## Form description

`Thank you for your interest in The Number Garden. This short form gives me a first sense of your family and what brings you here. After you submit it, I will follow up by email about next steps.`

## Questions

### 1. Parent or caregiver name

- Type: Short answer
- Required: Yes

### 2. Email address

- Type: Short answer
- Required: Yes

### 3. Phone number

- Type: Short answer
- Required: No
- Help text: `Optional, but helpful if text is the easiest way to coordinate.`

### 4. What brings your family to The Number Garden right now?

- Type: Paragraph
- Required: Yes
- Help text: `A few sentences is plenty. You might share what is drawing you here, what your family is looking for, or what kind of mathematical experience you hope to find.`

### 5. Which child or children are you thinking about right now?

- Type: Paragraph
- Required: Yes
- Help text: `You can include names, ages, and anything simple that would help me get oriented.`

### 6. What kind of support, challenge, or experience are you hoping for?

- Type: Checkboxes
- Required: Yes

Choices:

- More challenge
- More confidence and support
- A warmer relationship to math
- Richer mathematical experiences
- A better fit than school math currently provides
- Help rebuilding foundations
- A community for a math-loving child
- I am not quite sure yet

### 7. How did you hear about The Number Garden?

- Type: Multiple choice
- Required: Yes

Choices:

- Friend or family
- Teacher or educator
- Tutor or enrichment provider
- Homeschool community
- Neighborhood or local group
- Social media
- Web search
- Walked by the studio
- Other

### 8. What kind of next step would be most helpful?

- Type: Multiple choice
- Required: Yes

Choices:

- A short introductory conversation
- Information about upcoming openings
- Help thinking about fit for my family
- Information about scheduling and format

### 9. Anything else you would like to share?

- Type: Paragraph
- Required: No

## Confirmation message

`Thank you for reaching out. I’ll read your note and follow up by email soon. I’m beginning with a small founding group of families and appreciate your interest in The Number Garden.`

## Notes

- If you want the leanest possible version, questions 3 and 9 can be removed.
- The website should point to this form, not the deeper family profile form.
